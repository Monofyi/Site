Supply Chain the Bitecope Way!

Step 1

The plan or strategy

The supply chain manager tries to minimize shortages and keep costs down. The job is not only about logistics and purchasing inventory but also improve productivity, quality, and efficiency of operations.

Step 2

The source of raw materials or services

Manufacturer can co-ordinate with the distributors and produce required amount of products based on the real-time demand.

Step 3

Distribution

The distributor can make early decision about the stocks based on the re-order level if he gets to see real-time data updates about products.

Step 4

Delivery and logistics

The retailer can keep track of goods delivered, not delivered, excess as well as expired goods making warehouse management easier!

Step 5

The return system

Retailer can return excess goods or goods that are not sold to the distributor. Those good can be redirected to the required warehouse location.
